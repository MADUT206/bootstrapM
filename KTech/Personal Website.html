MADUT KUAJOK

Mr. Madut Kuajok, a South Sudanese individual, was born in the village of Magai, located in Kuac South Payam, Gogrial West County, Warrap State, South Sudan. His educational journey began at Karich Child Friendly School in April 2007, where he successfully completed his Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) in January 2014.

Continuing his education, Mr. Kuajok enrolled in Mayen Gumel Secondary School for his secondary education, and in 2016, he transferred to Kuajok National Secondary School for his Senior 2 studies. In pursuit of academic growth, he furthered his education at Kuajok Comprehensive College for Senior 3 in 2017. Demonstrating a strong work ethic and commitment to his studies, Mr. Kuajok successfully completed the South Sudan Secondary Certificate Examination (SSSCE) on December 24, 2018, showcasing his dedication to academic excellence.

Mr. Madut Kuajok's academic journey led him to be admitted to Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT) in B.Tech (IT) on September 10, 2019. He is currently pursuing his degree and has completed his 6th semester in the program, displaying commendable progress and dedication to his studies. Mr. Kuajok's accomplishments highlight his commitment to personal growth and excellence in the field of information technology.
